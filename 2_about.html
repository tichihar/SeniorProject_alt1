Hiroshige Ichihara

Since our company's founding in 1958, we have supported Japan's medical and physical sciences industries by manufacturing and selling products such as ultra-low temperature freezers and RI-compatible freeze dryers. Currently, we focus on selling slide organizers to medical institutions nationwide. All of our products are made domestically, with meticulous attention to detail in every component. We are proud to offer products that everyone can use safely.
